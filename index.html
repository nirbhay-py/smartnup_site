Frequently Asked Questions

For student developers interested in joining us.

  • What will I do as a student developer?

    You will get to work with like minded peers in the comfort of your house and be mentored through real-time projects that will ultimately be put to commercial use.

  • What will I get out of it?

    Upon completion of your project not only will you get a certificate and recommendation letter, but the title of an official EdTech open source developer. In addition, a share of the profits made through the commercialisation of your project will be shared.

  • I know how to program but don't think I can make software fit for commercial use. Do I still apply?

    Absolutely! For those of you are interested but not skilled enough, you have enough time to 'smartnUp'. Go on Udemy take a course and join the team. We would love to mentor young developers.

  • What are the time constraints?

    While there's no fixed amount of hours/week you need to put in, your projects do need to be ready by a certain date.
